# Homebody Practice --- Brand Spec Sheet

*Updated --- Cool Cozy Neutrals* "Rooted in connection --- meditation
for real life."

## Typography

### Primary Heading / Display

**Font:** Cormorant Garamond (serif)\
**Usage:** Logos, headers, section titles\
**Feel:** Organic, slightly calligraphic; evokes handcrafted calm\
**Color:** Deep Espresso --- `#2B241E`

### Body / Paragraphs

**Font:** Karla (sans-serif)\
**Usage:** Paragraphs, captions, buttons\
**Feel:** Humanist, rounded edges, excellent readability\
**Color:** Deep Espresso (90% opacity)\
**Notes:**\
- Avoid all caps\
- Use lowercase or simple title case for softness\
- Line height: 1.6--1.8\
- Generous letter spacing

## Color Palette --- Cool Cozy Neutrals

Essence: *Rooted, cozy, handcrafted calm. Feels like a warm drink on a
sunny morning and a cozy chair.*

### Palette Names

-   Soft Clay --- grounding neutral\
-   Warm Birch / Oat --- calm background tone\
-   Deep Ink / Espresso --- anchor text\
-   Pale Olive --- cool, subtle balance\
-   Muted Mineral Blue --- cool, subtle balance\
-   Ochre or Sun-warmed Coral --- small accents of vitality

## Mood & Essence

Warm, grounded, relational, handcrafted.\
Calm and tactile, with natural light, generous spacing, and textures
that suggest quiet presence.

> "Homebody is a space for remembering connection --- to yourself, your
> breath, and the quiet thread that runs through even the busiest days."
